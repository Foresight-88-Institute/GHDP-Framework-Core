## 1. Executive Summary: The Tempo Problem
**One Sentence Interpretation:** We thought we were growing, but we are actually overdrafting the battery of civilization.

We are facing a structural paradox:
* **GDP is rising** (Economic Velocity $\uparrow$)
* **GHDP is falling** (Civilizational Battery $\downarrow$)

This is the **"Great Divergence."**

## 2. The Great Divergence (GDP vs. GHDP)


[Image of economic growth curve]


* **Economic Output (GDP):** Measures the speed of extraction.
* **Human Capacity (GHDP):** Measures the sustainability of the workforce.
* **The Gap:** The widening distance between these two curves is "Tempo Debt."
