## 1. The Tempo Act Framework
A legislative framework to protect human rhythm from algorithmic acceleration.

## 2. Three Fundamental Time Rights
1.  **Right to Delay:** The legal protection to not respond immediately to digital stimuli.
2.  **Right to Disconnection:** The right to safeguard biological recovery time (sleep/rest).
3.  **Right to Focus:** Protection from algorithmic intrusion during deep work windows.

## 3. Algorithmic Tempo Audit (ATA)
Algorithms must be audited for "Acceleration Stress."
* **Metrics:** Interruption frequency, notification intensity, behavioral coercion.
* **Privacy:** Using **ZKTP (Zero-Knowledge Tempo Proofs)** to verify rhythm compliance without exposing user data.

## 4. Sector-Specific Rules
* **Tempo Workplace:** "No-Notification Zones" and "Deep Work Windows."
* **Tempo Schools:** Protecting youth cognitive bandwidth from early fragmentation.
