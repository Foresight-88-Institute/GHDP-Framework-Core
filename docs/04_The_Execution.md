# Chapter 17: Tempo Sandbox (The 100-Day Pilot)
**A National Pilot to Test, Measure, and Prototype the Future of Tempo Governance**

Tempo Governance, no matter how grand, must be validated through experimentation.
We designed the world's first **Tempo Sandbox**.
**Goal:** Not to build a perfect system immediately, but to:
* Test feasibility.
* Validate GHDP metrics.
* Identify high-return entry points.
* Prototype cross-ministry collaboration.

**Scope:** A 100-Day Pilot across three sectors:
1.  **Schools** (Child Rhythm)
2.  **Healthcare** (Physiological Recovery)
3.  **Workplace** (Deep Work)

---

## 1. Pilot A — Tempo Schools (The Deep Curriculum)
**Goal:** Establish early improvement models for **CBR** (Cognitive Bandwidth) and **PRA** (Physiological Rhythm).

### 17.1 Pilot Content
1.  **Deep Learning Blocks:** Minimum 2x daily sessions (45–60 mins) with zero digital interference.
2.  **Homework Rhythm Reform:** Shift from "Volume" to "Consolidation." No evening/weekend overload.
3.  **Natural Rhythm Hours:** Reintroduction of naps (primary) and reflection time (secondary).
4.  **Family Alignment:** Block school notifications after 7 PM.
5.  **Digital Tempo:** Mandatory "Deep Mode" on student devices.

### 17.2 Metrics
* **TSI:** Student autonomous time.
* **CBR:** Focus duration measurement.
* **PRA:** Sleep duration & HRV (via anonymous wearables).
* **RDV:** Family conflict reduction.
* **Teacher Rhythm Index:** Staff stress levels.

### 17.3 Expected Outcomes
* Sleep duration $\uparrow$ 15–30 mins.
* Significant increase in focus stamina.
* Reduction in behavioral conflicts.

---

## 2. Pilot B — Tempo Healthcare (The Buffer Zone)
**Goal:** Prototype improvements for **PRA** (Recovery) and **RDV** (Team Cohesion) in the most stressed sector.

### 17.4 Pilot Content
1.  **No-Push Protocol:** Ban non-essential administrative pushes during shifts.
2.  **Recovery Micro-Breaks:** Mandatory 5–7 min recovery slots every 2–3 hours.
3.  **Anonymous HRV Monitoring:** Using ZKTP to track ward-level fatigue without identifying individuals.
4.  **Deep Work Zones:** Protected time for surgery/diagnosis administrative work.
5.  **Emotional Support:** De-fragmented rest areas.

### 17.5 Metrics
* **PRA:** Collective HRV trends.
* **Error Rates:** Correlation with fatigue.
* **Burnout Index:** Shift-based fatigue scores.
* **Team Cohesion:** RDV scores.

### 17.6 Expected Outcomes
* Reduction in medical errors.
* Lower night-shift fatigue.
* Higher team morale.

---

## 3. Pilot C — Tempo Workplace (The Deep Work Trial)
**Goal:** Elevate **TSI** (Time Sovereignty) and **Innovation Yield**.

### 17.7 Pilot Content
1.  **No-Push Workplace:** "Rhythm Mode" for enterprise AI.
2.  **Deep Work Blocks:** 3x weekly 90-min protected sessions.
3.  **Meeting Rhythm Protocol:** Cap on meeting hours; mandatory agendas.
4.  **Local-First Focus Mode:** Device-level push freezing.
5.  **Tempo HR Policy:** Workload caps based on PRA thresholds.

### 17.8 Metrics
* **CBR:** Cognitive Bandwidth recovery.
* **Innovation Yield:** Quality of output per hour.
* **Task Switching:** Frequency reduction.
* **Retention:** Correlation with rhythm satisfaction.

### 17.9 Expected Outcomes
* "Quality Productivity" replaces "Busy Productivity."
* Lower error rates in complex tasks.
* Improved work-life balance (TSI).
